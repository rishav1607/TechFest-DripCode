How Karma Works

Four simple steps to outsmart scammers

01

Scammer Detection

Our AI analyzes incoming calls in real-time, identifying scam patterns, suspicious keywords, and known fraud tactics using advanced machine learning.

02

AI "Granny" Takes Over

Once detected, our AI persona — a sweet, confused elderly woman — engages the scammer in conversation, keeping them occupied and away from real victims.

03

Time Wasting Tactics

Granny asks them to repeat, claims hearing problems, goes on tangents about her grandchildren, and creates realistic delays that frustrate scammers.

04

Data Collection & Reporting

While keeping them busy, we gather intelligence: phone numbers, scripts, tactics, and locations. This data is shared with authorities to shut down operations.
